Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a beloved Italian dish characterized by its layers of rich, hearty ingredients. At its core, lasagna features sheets of pasta, often slightly wavy, which are alternated with layers of savory meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and a generous sprinkling of mozzarella or Parmesan cheese. The meat sauce is typically made from ground beef or pork, simmered with tomatoes, garlic, onions, and a blend of aromatic herbs like basil and oregano. As it bakes, the layers meld together, creating a deliciously gooey, bubbling concoction with a crispy, golden top. Each bite offers a comforting blend of flavors and textures, making lasagna a quintessentially satisfying and indulgent dish that’s perfect for gatherings and family meals.

  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
